What to Expect:

Too many individuals this day and age find themselves just trying to get through day-by-day. They are trying to cope with having depression and anxiety. However, they do not want to have the stigma that comes with seeking professional help. They do not want the solution to be pills. The process of trying to find the right prescription and then the right dose can take up a lot of precious time. Time they might not have. Do you find yourself in this situation? This site offers another solution. This site is specifically for people that are trying to find information and learn or to find a community and be inspired by others that are going through what you are going through. Here, you will find answered questions that individuals may have from being undiagnosed. At first, the focus will be mainly on depression and anxiety but will branch out as there is need.

Specifics:

The site will give you the opportunity to find information that can inspire you. It will also give you an opportunity to join a community, via a mailing list, that will connect you to others around the world that are dealing with what you are. Lastly, this site will give you an opportunity to fill out a form that will allow you to comment on our site. This way, what is missing from this site and/or wanted can be added.

Why Use This Website Over Others:

This site will not only provide you with information but also a community where you can learn from others going through what you are on a personal level. It is specifically designed for people that are not diagnosed and do not want to find a solution to their suffering via medicine. This page provides the information to read about depression and anxiety as well as giving a brief overview of the purpose of the site. Hopefully, you can find something from these pages of relevance to you.
