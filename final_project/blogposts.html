Straight From The Spouce

December 3, 2021

I have been living with a spouce with depression for 9 years. This has not been an easy road for anyone. It is hard to watch someone you love suffer and not be able to help. There were times, many times, when I was trying to help but did just the opposite. I was soon the only one he felt he could talk to but I also had a very hard time hearing how he was feeling. He tried many different avenues to feel better (doctors, prescriptions, therapists, therapy practices, meditation, etc) to no avail. Again, so hard to watch when he was trying as best he could and still received no help or relief. He never gave up though. He kept getting up everyday and trying his best. Though some days are better than others, he has learned how to keep going and living with depression. Sometimes it is hard to remember (especially on the low days) how much of a hero my husband is to me. While depression is hard for everyone involved, hardest for the one suffering from depression, through faith, love, and long-suffering all involved can keep going. To those still struggling, please don't give up. You are needed and loved!
