Overview

Purpose

Have you ever felt misunderstood? Have you ever sought help and still felt lost? Do you need a place to go to find hope? That is the purpose of this website. Not to get gain or to sell anything but to be a place of information. To be a place where anyone can go for ideas on how to improve their life. This website is meant to provide a sense of community and resources that can help everyone live a life they can be physically, emotionally, mentally, and spiritually happy living.

Audience

The target audience for this website is mainly for people looking for help concerning their health. So many suffer with diseases like anxiety and depression and go to a doctor for help only to have nothing solved. This site is also going to be for those looking for answers that haven't sought professional advice. This site, however, will not be a place for people to go to be diagnosed, rather it is a site for our audience to be able to come to and find help regardless of where they are on their personal health journey.

Typography

Heading Font: Fira Sans (google font), Georgia, serif

Picked this font because it is more bold, susinct, clear, and takes up a bit more space. This is also a calming and simplistic font which is the end goal for this site. Felt it was important to pick a font that was a little more bold than the paragraph so the heading would stand out a bit. Also, picked two fonts (for the heading and paragraphs) that are similar so as not to distract from the information on the site.

Paragraph Font: Ubuntu (google font), Trebuchet MS, sans-serif

Wanted to keep the paragraph font simple and easy to read. The idea is to have a calming affect and felt this font would do this best. Want the audience to be able to read what is in the paragraphs quickly and easily without really having to concentrate hard (which would have to be done with a hard to read font).

Content

Home page

  • What to Expect: Too many individuals this day and age find themselves just trying to get through day-by-day. They are trying to cope with having depression and anxiety. However, they do not want to have the stigma that comes with seeking professional help. They do not want the solution to be pills. The process of trying to find the right prescription and then the right dose can take up a lot of precious time. Time they might not have. This site offers another solution. This site is specifically for people that are trying to find information and learn or to find a community and be inspired by others that are going through what they are going through. The site will answer questions that individuals may have from being undiagnosed. It will focus mainly on depression and anxiety and branch out as there is need.
  • Specifics: The site will give visitors the opportunity to find information that can inspire them. It will also give them an opportunity to join a community via a mailing list that will connect them to others around the world that are dealing with what they are. Lastly, this site will give visitors an opportunity to fill out a form that will allow them to comment on our site that will be posted right on the website.
  • Why Use This Webpage Over Others: Visitors will come to this site and not others because it not only will provide them with information but also a community where they can learn from others going through what they are on a personal level. It is specifically designed for people that are not diagnosed and do not want to find a solution to their suffering via medicine. The home page will provide the information and a brief overview of the purpose of the site. Hopefully directing any site visitors to the page(s) of most relevance for them.

Blog Posts

The second child page will be where the visitors will be able to read blog posts that reference popular content [including articles] which provide a series of approaches and unique methods to empower guests towards developing their own resilience. In addition to gaining access to meaningful [readable] content, including some special posts developed by guests, site guests will also have the ability to enter their information (via a form) to opt-in to a mailing list that provides regular updates of new site content. The visitor of the website will fill out their name (or an anonymous name), their email, and their comment or blog about our website.
