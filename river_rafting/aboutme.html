I have many hobbies. I am currently a stay at home mom pursuing a higher education. I got my Degree in Administrative Assistance but that was 17 years ago. I was directed to go back to school. I finished the Pathway Connect Program and loved it and am now looking to get a BS by pursuing three certificates; Coding and Web Design, CADD, and Graphic Design. I have decided to follow this career path because I can work from home while still having the freedom to be home when my kids and grandkids are. Also, because all of those certificate topics facinate me. My husband is very excited for when I graduate so I can fulfill all his grand plans with starting businesses.

I feel very blessed to have been able to be a member all my life. One of my favourite quotes is by Elder Russell M. Nelson: Men are that they might have joy - not guilt trips!
